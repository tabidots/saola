# Privacy Policy for SaoLa Dictionary Extension

Last updated: 18 Dec 2025

## Data Collection

SaoLa Dictionary does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data or browsing information.

## Local Storage

The extension stores user preferences (theme, pronunciation display, dialect preference) locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device.

## Webpage Content

While the extension reads Vietnamese text on webpages you visit to provide dictionary definitions, it does not record, transmit, or store any webpage content.

## Third-Party Services

The extension does not communicate with any external servers or third-party services. All dictionary data is bundled with the extension.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact [tabidots@gmail.com](mailto:tabidots@gmail.com).
