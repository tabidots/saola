## About SaoLa

**SaoLa** is a Vietnamese-English dictionary designed specifically for learners of Vietnamese. In addition to helping learners expand your vocabulary, it is also intended to improve pronunciation and the ability to recognize words in speech—the foundational elements of conversational fluency.

### Features

- **Audio for common words:** Hear frequent words spoken by state-of-the-art Northern and Southern Vietnamese AI voices
- **Dual-dialect phonetic guides:** A visual tool to make some pronunciation details clearer
- **Frequency rankings:** Learn the most useful words first
- **Smart search:** Look up Vietnamese or English, no need to type special Vietnamese letters or tone marks
- **Mobile-friendly:** Works on any device, no installation needed (coming soon: offline mode!)

### Data Sources

Dictionary data is sourced from [English Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org) via [Kaikki.org](https://kaikki.org).

Frequncy data is based on the 1M sentence mixed-source corpus from [Leipzig Wortschatz](https://wortschatz.uni-leipzig.de/en/download/).

The current audio files were generated using [Viettel AI](http://viettelai.vn)'s text-to-speech service. Better audio and greater word coverage coming soon!

### Privacy

SaoLa runs entirely in your browser. No data is collected or sent to any server. Your search history is stored locally and never leaves your device.
