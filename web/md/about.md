## About SaoLa

**SaoLa** is a Vietnamese-English dictionary designed specifically for learners of Vietnamese. In addition to helping you expand your vocabulary, it is also intended to improve pronunciation and the ability to recognize words in speech—the foundational elements of conversational fluency.

### Features

- **Audio for common words:** Hear frequent words spoken by state-of-the-art Northern and Southern Vietnamese AI voices
- **Dual-dialect phonetic guides:** A visual tool to make some pronunciation details clearer
- **Frequency rankings:** Learn the most useful words first
- **Smart search:** Look up Vietnamese or English, no need to type special Vietnamese letters or tone marks
- **Mobile-friendly:** Works on any device, no installation needed (coming soon: offline mode!)

### Data Sources

Dictionary data is sourced from [English Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org) via [Kaikki.org](https://kaikki.org).

The word frequency data in this dictionary is derived from two corpora:

#### Leipzig Wortschatz Mixed Corpus (1M sentences)

Provided by the University of Leipzig.
Source: [Leipzig Wortschatz](https://wortschatz.uni-leipzig.de/en/download/)

#### OpenSubtitles Dataset (English–Vietnamese)

Original dataset described in:

P. Lison & J. Tiedemann (2016). *OpenSubtitles2016: Extracting Large Parallel Corpora from Movie and TV Subtitles.* Proceedings of LREC 2016.

Accessed through the processed release maintained at [this GitHub repo](https://github.com/sinhngn/English-Vietnamese-parallel-corpus-context).

The underlying data originates from the OpenSubtitles collection and is redistributed according to the project’s licensing terms.

### Privacy

SaoLa runs entirely in your browser. No data is collected or sent to any server. Your search history is stored locally and never leaves your device.
